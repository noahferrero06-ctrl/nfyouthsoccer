Meet Your Coach

About Coach Noah

I grew up playing AYSO in the Pacific Palisades, where my love for soccer first began. My dad was my first ever coach, helping shape my early passion for the game.

I later advanced to competitive clubs including LA Bulls and LA Breakers. While playing for LA Breakers, I was awarded Player of the Year for 2022 and 2023. Then in 2024, I was awarded Player of the Year at Palisades Charter High School, and pictured in the bottom right, I am holding my Player of the Year award with my parents beside me, who were always there cheering me on every step of the way.

After becoming a certified coach and referee in 2022, I went on to earn my D License and became a coach at LA Bulls, my former club. Now I coach for the same team that helped shape me as a player, passing on the same values, work ethic, and love for the game that my own coaches instilled in me.
